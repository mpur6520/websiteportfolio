EARSKETCH RINGTONE

Using Python and Earsketch, create loops of soundtracks into a coherent piece someone may select when choosing their ringtone. The overall product is meant to be a cohesive musical piece created with Python code—using loops, variables, and functions.

This was my first time interacting with Earsketch and Python to create music. I didn’t have any exact inspiration—the sounds I chose were ones I thought would sound good together and I went through a lot of trial and error. I’m proud and surprised that I understood the program so easily, the functions were intuitive to me despite the difficulty of creating something coherent. I’ve referred back to this countless times when using Earsketch to check how and where I did things right.
